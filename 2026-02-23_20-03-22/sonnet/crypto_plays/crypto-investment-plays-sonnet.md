## Risk Management Guidelines

### Position Sizing Rules
1. Never allocate more than 12% to any single asset
2. Speculative plays (meme coins, presales) max 3% each
3. Keep 5-10% in stablecoins for opportunities
4. Rebalance monthly or after 50% moves

### Stop Loss Discipline
1. Set stop losses immediately after entry
2. Use -10% for established projects
3. Use -20% to -40% for high-volatility plays
4. Never move stop loss lower (only higher)

### Profit Taking Strategy
1. Scale out progressively: 25% at Target 1, 30% at Target 2, etc.
2. Always secure initial investment at 2x
3. Hold 20-30% "moon bags" for home runs
4. Rebalance winners back to original allocation

### Portfolio Rebalancing
1. Monthly review of all positions
2. Trim winners above 15% portfolio weight
3. Add to losers only if thesis unchanged
4. Cut positions if thesis breaks (don't average down blindly)
