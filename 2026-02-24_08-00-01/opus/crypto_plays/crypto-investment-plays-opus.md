## Risk Management Rules

1. Never invest more than you can afford to lose entirely.
2. Honor stop losses -- when hit, exit. Do not average down below your stop.
3. DCA into positions -- do not deploy 100% of capital at once.
4. Scale out profits at each TP level.
5. Keep speculative plays small (1-3% of portfolio).
6. Review weekly -- re-evaluate if key supports break.
7. If BTC breaks $55,000, reassess ALL positions.
8. Track transactions for Dutch tax reporting (Box 3).
